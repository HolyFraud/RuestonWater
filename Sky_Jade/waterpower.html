Absolute Purification

Botanical water is extracted from plants and purified by nature, it is free of toxic chemicals and can effectively avoid radioactive or toxic elements from building up in human body.

Contains 74 plant-derived minerals and macro minerals that are beneficial to human body and the rare types of water molecules that only exist in botanical water are useful in optimising hydration.

Sustainability

Botanical water is sustainable as we make full use of fruit and vegetables and these replenish annually as we grow food.

Vegan Friendly

It is suitable for vegetarians and vegans because the water source are fruits and vegetables, hence no animal constituents in the water. Unlike other water through flooding and rain, which ultimately make contact with decaying animals
